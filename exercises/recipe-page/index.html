Gochujang Skewers


Prep Time: 15 Minutes Cook Time: 15 Minutes

Total Time: 30 Minutes Yeilds: 6 Skewers

If you’re looking to spice up your usual chicken and rice routine, these Korean gochujang chicken skewers are a game changer. With a sweet and spicy marinade that comes together in minutes, they’re cooked to perfection in the air fryer (or oven or BBQ if that’s more your vibe). It’s a simple yet bold way to bring something exciting to the table without all the fuss.
